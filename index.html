A Story of Marry

It’s a beautiful weekend morning, Mary goes into a Coffee shop , there’re so many people in line and wait to check out with cash.

However, the shop’s beacon device identified Mary’s favorite taste as quickly as she stepped in, Mary got her Coffee soon and checked out with her phone and went out with a happy mode.

  • Contactless
  • Fast payment

Mary drives to the Parking of shopping mall, luckily, there’re beacon devices in this Parking.

As soon as Mary entered, her phone app gets connected with the beacon devices and gave Mary voice navigation and lead her to an available parking space directly.

  • Voice navigation
  • Indoor navigation

The shopping mall beacon devices identified Mary’s app and analyzed Mary’s shopping habits and pushing her discount information of her favorite brand “Zala”---50% DISCOUNT!

Mary went to Zala shoppe directly and bought her favorite shoes!

Get the best discount of your favorite brands the moment you stepped into a shopping mall.

  • Behavior analysis

It says “Buy steak, juice for free”. Mary is so happy to see this coupon and went into the restaurant and ordered her favorite steak.
